Event

The CTBUH holds an annual Tall + Urban Innovation Conference, where Award of Excellence winners present their projects to an international audience and live juries. Overall category winners and Audience Award winners are selected during the conference and awards are conferred at the ceremony and dinner held at the close of the event.
